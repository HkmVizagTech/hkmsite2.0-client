Janmashtami Mahotsav 2026

श्री कृष्ण जन्माष्टमी महोत्सव

Janmashtami marks the divine appearance day of Lord Sri Krishna — the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Celebrated with unparalleled devotion at Hare Krishna Vaikuntham, the entire temple transforms into a sacred Vrindavan with elaborate decorations, continuous kirtan and abhishekam.

Programme Highlights

  • 6:00 AM — Mangala Aarti & Special Darshan
  • 10:00 AM — Bhagavad Gita discourse
  • 6:30 PM — Cultural performances & kirtan
  • 10:00 PM — Maha-abhishekam of Sri Krishna
  • 12:00 AM — Midnight aarti & mahaprasadam

What to Expect

Experience continuous bhajans, traditional decorations, beautifully attired deities, dramatic enactments of Krishna's pastimes, and a grand mahaprasadam distribution. Devotees observe a fast until midnight, breaking it only after the abhishekam ceremony.

Sponsor Special Items

Devotees are invited to sponsor special offerings — silver thrones, gold ornaments, garland sets, festival cakes, and bhog preparations. Each sponsorship comes with a personalized acknowledgment and prasadam at home.

Welcome to Hare Krishna Vaikuntham

Hare Krishna Vaikuntham, the spiritual and cultural initiative of Hare Krishna Movement Visakhapatnam, lovingly known as the "Hare Krishna Mandir," aspires to uplift society through the timeless teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, along with the authentic practices of Vedic spiritual culture.

Srila Prabhupada, the Founder-Acharya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, stated, "Unless you change the society, how can you make social welfare?"

Hare Krishna Vaikuntham · Biggest Temple of Vizag

Hare Krishna Vaikuntham is the largest temple cum cultural complex in Vizag with a grand footprint of 3.5 lakh square feet area. The complex is built with the vision to nurture and preserve the ancient divine culture of Vedic India. Through the multiple initiatives of the Centre, we provide opportunities to inculcate cultural values.

Vizag — the spiritual heart of Andhra Pradesh

The coastal city of Visakhapatnam holds a deep spiritual significance — home to the ancient Simhachalam temple of Lord Narasimha, and a long tradition of Krishna devotion. Hare Krishna Vaikuntham extends this sacred legacy, building a magnificent abode for Lord Sri Krishna.

Continuing Srila Prabhupada's vision

Hare Krishna Movement is building this biggest temple cum cultural complex to fulfill the desires of Srila Prabhupada and amplify the glories of Lord Krishna. The Centre will facilitate cow protection, propagate the Hare Krishna sankirtan, educate the society on the basis of Vedic principles from Srimad Bhagavad-Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, distribute sanctified food among the needy, and expand the culture through multiple projects.

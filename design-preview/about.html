The Story of Hare Krishna Vaikuntham

A mission to revive devotion and spiritual culture in Visakhapatnam.

The Hare Krishna Movement in Vizag began with a simple yet powerful purpose — to deliver the highest knowledge of Vedic scriptures like the Bhagavad-Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

By following the core philosophies of these sacred texts preserved through the Gaudiya Vaishnav disciplic lineage, our guiding practice offers devotees a practical path to attain spiritual growth and devotion in today's modern lifestyle.

Daily Spiritual Practices We Follow:

Chanting Hare Krishna Maha-Mantra
Studying the Vedic Scriptures
Honoring sanctified prasadam
Serving the holy devotees

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Spread Krishna consciousness through inspirational programs, deep spiritual learning, and authentic Vedic culture — touching every life in Vizag and beyond with the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad-Gita.

Mission

Build a grand cultural and spiritual center — Hare Krishna Vaikuntham — where every visitor experiences the divine, finds peace, and reconnects with India's eternal spiritual heritage through devotion and service.
